# Thinking Log

- Region-based and material-based multipliers

## Pricing Logic

- Region multipliers: US 1.0, EU 1.15, Asia 0.9
- Material multipliers: Aluminum 1.0, Steel 0.85, Carbon 1.5, Leather 1.2
